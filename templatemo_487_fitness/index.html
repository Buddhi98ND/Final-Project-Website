Why should people donate blood? Safe blood saves lives. Blood is needed by women with complications during pregnancy and childbirth, children with severe anaemia, often resulting from malaria or malnutrition, accident victims and surgical and cancer patients.

About The Research

The proposed application, "Donor Me," was developed to solve the above issue of managing blood and organ donations effectively in Sri Lanka. Also, this solution can be used for any country with minor modifications. "Donor Me" is a mobile application that is an effective blood and organ donation system.
